Hola

Aug 18th, 2013

I’ve been in Ecuador for about three weeks, in which time I’ve managed to help plan our start-of-office immersion program, deliver a presentation on software testing in Spanish and visit Mindo, a small town in the jungle about two hours outside of Quito.

While in Mindo, we rode a truck-engine powered gondola over the forest, arriving on the other side of the gorge for a hike through the forest.

We also saw hummingbirds.
